Mongolia is changing all its addresses to three-word phrases

Mongolia will become a global pioneer next month, when its national post office starts referring to locations by a series of three-word phrases instead of house numbers and street names. The new system is devised by a British startup called What3Words, which has assigned a three-word phrase to every point on the globe.
