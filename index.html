Decoupling Content Management in Drupal

Decoupled content management is a new way to look at the process of editing and managing content on the Web. Traditional Content Management Systems tend to tie their users to a built-in content storage and editing interface. The concept of decoupled content management is about a clean separation of concerns between the content repository, the web framework, and the web editing tool.
