New Hubble Portrait of Mars

Hubble Space Telescope took a photo of Mars on May 12, 2016, when Mars was 50 million miles from Earth, revealing details as small as 20 to 30 miles across. This observation was made just a few days before Mars opposition, when the sun and Mars will be on exact opposite sides of Earth, and when Mars will be at a distance of 47.4 million miles.
