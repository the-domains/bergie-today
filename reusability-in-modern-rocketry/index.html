Reusability in Modern Rocketry

Today, if you want to go to space, there are four main launch providers (AKA rocket companies). These are Roscosmos, United Launch Alliance, Space Exploration Technologies Inc. and Arianespace. Over these there are fifteen (I keep remembering more) launchers (rockets), of which five have yet to be implemented.
