Google is making it easy to digitize and share old photos with a new app called PhotoScan - just in time to embarrass you for the holidays. PhotoScan is an app that lets you take a picture of a physical, old photo. While the user interface is simple enough for anyone to use, there’s a great deal going on in the background. The app uses machine learning to remove glares, crop out the desk or wall behind the printed photo, and make sure the colors are accurate so that you get a perfect scan.
