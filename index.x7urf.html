The fastest-growing mobile phone markets barely use apps

Africa and Asia, the two fastest growing mobile markets, aren't very big on apps. The overwhelming majority of mobile internet activity in the regions is spent on web pages, according to a report released yesterday (July 28) by Opera Mediaworks. In Asia and Africa, websites made up 90% and 96% of mobile impressions, respectively, in the second quarter.
